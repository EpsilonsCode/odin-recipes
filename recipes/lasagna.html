Student's Lasagna

Description

Easy to make and wonerful in flavour - the student's lasagna should satisfy both your tongue and your wallet

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Hard cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef until its brown
  2. Mix it with the sauce
  3. Shred your hard cheese of choice
  4. Put everything in layers in a baking vessel
  5. Bake until its hot
